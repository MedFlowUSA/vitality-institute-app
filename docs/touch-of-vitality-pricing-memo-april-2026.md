# Touch of Vitality Pricing Memo

Date: April 6, 2026

Prepared for: Jane

Purpose: align Touch of Vitality public and internal pricing with realistic Los Angeles market expectations while preserving a premium, competitive position.

## Pricing Position

Touch of Vitality should sit in the middle of the Los Angeles market:

- above discount med spa pricing
- below ultra-premium Beverly Hills luxury pricing
- strong enough to signal provider-led care, quality, and brand credibility

This pricing model is designed to feel fair, current, and sustainable while still converting well.

## Internal Guidance

Use this framing when discussing pricing:

"Touch of Vitality pricing is designed to be competitive for Los Angeles while still reflecting a premium, provider-guided experience."

"We are not positioning the brand as bargain med spa pricing. We are positioning it as fair, credible, and high-quality."

"Entry pricing is meant to reduce friction, while monthly plans and bundles support retention and long-term value."

## Why This Works

- Consult pricing is high enough to feel professional and medically guided.
- GLP-1 pricing stays competitive in a crowded market without appearing cheap.
- Hormone and peptide pricing feels premium but still accessible.
- Botox pricing stays within believable Los Angeles norms.
- Bundles create room for stronger retained revenue without forcing every patient into the top tier.
- Add-ons are priced low enough to improve attach rate.

## Suggested Public Positioning

- Personalized provider-led care
- Premium wellness without luxury-clinic overpricing
- Fair Los Angeles pricing for ongoing optimization and aesthetics
- Clear entry points with stronger premium tiers for high-touch support
