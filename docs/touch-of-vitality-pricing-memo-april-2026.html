Pricing Position

Touch of Vitality should sit above discount med spa pricing and below ultra-premium Beverly Hills luxury pricing. The goal is to feel credible, current, and clinically grounded without looking inexpensive or overinflated.

How To Position It

"Touch of Vitality pricing is designed to be competitive for Los Angeles while still reflecting a premium, provider-guided experience."

"We are not positioning the brand as bargain med spa pricing. We are positioning it as fair, credible, and high-quality."

Why This Works

  • Consults feel professional and medically guided.
  • GLP-1 stays competitive without looking cheap.
  • Hormone and peptide pricing remains premium but accessible.
  • Add-ons support conversion and bundle growth.

Suggested Public Positioning

  • Personalized provider-led care
  • Premium wellness without luxury-clinic overpricing
  • Fair Los Angeles pricing for optimization and aesthetics
  • Clear entry points with stronger premium tiers for high-touch support

Operational Notes

  • Public marketing prices and internal service prices were updated together.
  • This pricing should be reviewed again after conversion and retention data accumulates.
  • Monthly hormone pricing remains partly inferred because many clinics publish consultations and procedures more clearly than memberships.
